About Us

ReviveCart is a unique platform that helps reduce food wastage by selling near-expiry products at discounted prices. We aim to provide affordable food options while supporting sustainability.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between surplus food and consumers who need it, promoting sustainability and affordability. By purchasing from ReviveCart, you help reduce waste and contribute to a greener planet.
